Google Maps to BMW NBT converter  

1. Share a map from https://www.google.com/maps/d/ with at least "Anyone who has the link can view" permission

2. Copy a link to share

3. Paste the link in a field below

4. Press "Convert"

5. Save archive

6. Unpack contents to the root of a USB stick

7. Import routes in a car
